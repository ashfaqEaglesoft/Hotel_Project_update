Why Choose Us

Why choose us? We investigated among our guests feedback to find out what they most appreciated during their journey and here we are with almost 5 good reasons to come to visit us at MST Hotels Siracusa.

  1. Our main goal is to let you live an exciting and unforgettable holiday creating customized itineraries according to your preferences. We are glad to create with our guests friendly relationship and to share smiles and small talks. We never forget what Paolo likes and what bothers Sophia, we would really satisfy your requests and even anticipate your wishes.
  2. Our guests tell about us: feel like heaven – oasis of peace – beautiful, it seems like it has a real soul. We are really pleased of this as we put our heart and soul in our work, we try to pay attention to every detail and to assure to our rooms the best cleaning day by day.
  3. About our breakfasts: looking at our buffet, many guests shout out “it’s really a lot” and they smiled. Maybe Lucia, the breakfast’s chef, will take me to the failure but we both claim that otherwise the buffet would be sad. Our chef strikes me for being so modest and helpful, he never says ‘no’ to our guests requests. People congratulate him on his cooking and he always blushes. Every morning, at the daybreak, we go together to the market, just to be sure to have the freshest fish, the best cheeses and the most delicious fruits. Our friendly waiters let your meals even more pleasant.
  4. If you like living in the city you are not looking for Hotel Borgo Pantano, otherwise, if you wish to enjoy the peace and the nature come to visit us. Our hotel is at just 7 Km far from the inner city, you can enjoy the beautiful swimming pool and relax on the sun beds. No hurry to look for a parking. We would suggest you to rent a car to explore the beautiful landscapes, otherwise the shuttle service is available (from Euro 5,00) and it takes just 12 minutes to reach the city.
  5. That’s our motto. Last January, Trip advisor rewarded us with the Travellers’ Choice 2018 which acknowledges us the 9th place for the best Italian Hotels Top 25. Luigi, the hotel manager, put big banners of the awards on our website and on the social pages and I bended over with laughter for this. I asked him for using smaller banners but he claimed “We put heart and soul in what we do and our hospitality deserves to be underlined”. We are so excited and happy, even worried!
